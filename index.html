Bio

Normal Human Reaction is a Sarasota, FL based indie rock project blending dense guitars, spacey atmospheres, and understated melodies. Drawing from 90s shoegaze and early 2000s alternative, the band focuses on mood, texture, and emotional restraint rather than excess. Crowd focused and preformace driven to draw attention and bring energy with each song.
